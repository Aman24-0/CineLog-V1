# 🎬 Cinelog - Ultimate Edition
### Personal Cinematic Command Center

Cinelog ek high-end, premium movie aur TV show tracking vault hai. Ise khaas taur par entertainment journey ko organize aur streamline karne ke liye design kiya gaya hai, jisme discovery, detailed tracking aur instant streaming ek hi aesthetic interface mein milte hain.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🗄️ Universal Vault & Smart Tracking

- **Unified Tracking:** Movies aur TV shows dono ko ek hi jagah manage karne ki suvidha.
- **Status Management:** Har title ko `Planned`, `Watching`, ya `Completed` status mein categorize karne ka system.
- **Advanced Progress Tracker:** TV series ke liye dedicated Season aur Episode wise tracking, jisme `+1 Ep` quick update button aur visual progress bar shamil hai.
- **Personalized Tags:** Movies ko custom tags (jaise: `Theatre`, `Laptop`, `Family`) ke saath mark karne ki capability, jisse vault mein search aur filter karna aasaan ho jata hai.
- **Watch History:** Calendar support ke saath exact Watch Date save karne ka option.

---

### 🎥 6-Node Streaming Matrix

- **One-Click Streaming:** App ke andar hi inbuilt player ke saath movies aur shows dekhne ki suvidha.
- **Multi-Server Switcher:** 6 premium servers — VidZee, VidLink, Vidsrc.ru, Embed.su, Vidsrc.cc, aur AutoEmbed — ka cluster, jisse English ke saath-sath Hindi aur regional dubbed content milne ke chances 100% rehte hain.
- **On-the-Fly Switching:** Video player ke upar hi dropdown switcher, jisse bina player close kiye turant node badla ja sake.

---

### ⭐ Triple Rating & Metadata

- **Real-time Insights:** TMDB integration ke zariye synopsis, posters, trailers aur cast/crew ki photo-rich profiles.
- **IMDb & Rotten Tomatoes 🍅:** OMDb API ke zariye har title ki official IMDb aur Rotten Tomatoes ratings automatically fetch hoti hain.
- **Sage's Rating:** Har title par apna personal rating (1–10) aur custom notes dene ka section.

---

### 📅 Upcoming Radar (30-Day Timeline)

- **Future Scan:** Agle 30 dinon mein release hone wali movies aur episodes ka chronological vertical view.
- **Smart Filters:** Indian vs International content ke beech switch karne aur language-wise (Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, etc.) filter karne ki facility.
- **Curated Content:** Reality shows aur non-scripted programs ko block karke sirf premium web series aur movies par focus karne wala smart logic.

---

### 📂 Smart Lists & Franchises

- **Collection Folders:** Movies ko franchises (jaise: MCU, Batman, John Wick) ke folders mein organize karne ka feature.
- **Visual Backgrounds:** Folders par automatically unke andar ki pehli movie ka backdrop image dikhta hai.
- **Manual Reordering:** Titles ko timeline ya apni pasand ke custom order mein up-down arrows ke zariye set karne ki freedom.

---

## 💎 Design & Aesthetics

### 🎨 Premium UI/UX

- **Glassmorphic Design:** Purane boring layouts ki jagah modern blurred surfaces aur cinematic animations.
- **7 Multi-Theme Support:** 7 premium themes — Sage, Matrix, Netflix, Cyberpunk, Interstellar, Neon Horizon, aur Vibranium — jo poore app ka look ek click mein badal deti hain.
- **Infinity Ring Insights:** Ek aesthetic circular progress bar jo total watch-time (days aur hours mein) aur top genres ka visualization dikhata hai.
- **Cinelog Wrapped:** Saali ki movie journey ka ek cinematic summary card generate karne ka feature.

---

## 📱 Performance & Connectivity

- **PWA Ready:** App ko mobile ya PC par install karke full-screen native feel ke saath use karne ki facility.
- **Fast Loading:** Optimized CSS loading screen jo heavy images ke bina bhi premium cinematic feel deti hai.
- **Data Sync:** Poore vault data ko JSON ya CSV format mein export karke backup rakhne aur dobara import karne ka secure system.
